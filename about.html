Our Story

From a Small Garage to Industry Leader

Stackly was founded in 2010 by a group of designers who believed that packaging should be more than just a container — it should be an experience. What started as a small operation in a garage has grown into a global packaging solutions provider serving over 15,000 clients across 50 countries.

Our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of quality, sustainability, and innovation. We've pioneered eco-friendly packaging solutions, invested in cutting-edge printing technology, and built a team of passionate experts who share our vision.
